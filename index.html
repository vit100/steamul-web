About Us

Welcome to Steamul Sauna, a family-owned sanctuary of relaxation established in 2010. Born from our profound passion for authentic sauna traditions, we've created a haven where health and rejuvenation flourish.

Our mission extends beyond providing exceptional thermal experiences—we're dedicated to promoting healthy lifestyles, honoring traditional family values, and ensuring every visitor leaves with a sense of renewal and satisfaction.

Today, Steamul has blossomed into a beloved destination that draws sauna enthusiasts from across the Greater Toronto Area, throughout Ontario, and even across the border from the United States. What brings them here? The perfect blend of authentic Eastern European bathhouse traditions and our unwavering commitment to exceptional service.

Step into our world where ancient sauna wisdom meets modern comfort, and experience why we've become the premier destination for those seeking genuine thermal therapy and relaxation.

Sauna Common Questions and Answers

What are the benefits of sauna use?

- Detoxification of the body through sweating
- Stress reduction and mental relaxation
- Improved physical and mental wellbeing
- Support for weight management
- Enhanced blood circulation
- Strengthened immune system
- Accelerated recovery after physical activity
- Improved athletic performance and endurance

What is included in my visit price?

Entrance fee includes a bathrobe, 3 towels, a locker, tea, purified drinking water and NO TIME LIMIT during business hours Monday through Thursday. 4 hours maximum limit on Friday through Sunday. $10 an hour for each full or partial hour above limit.

What should I bring for sauna session?

You need to have a bathing suit (for women) or shorts (for men), flip-flops. We recommend you have a woolen hat to prevent your head from overheating. If you don’t have one you can buy it from us.

How should I use sauna for my best benefit?

Individual heat tolerance varies. Listen to your feelings. If some people could be inside for 10-15 min, for others 1-2 min could be enough. We recommend start with warm shower and proceed with first short (5-7 min) session, then take warm shower again. Relax for 10-15 min, drink water or tea. Repeat sauna session 3-4 times with 10-15 min breaks. After second session you can start using contrast shower or cold water overhead buckets eventually going to cold water plunge. Drink a lot of water since your body gets dehydrated. Take your time and relax.

What is Halo therapy?

Halotherapy sessions last 45 minutes inside our Salt Palace room. Guests relax in reclining chairs and enjoy the beautiful glowing pink salt walls while calming music floats through the salt enriched air. The healing effect is increased with use of special state-of-the-art halo generator, which injects a dry salt aerosol into the salt room so you can realize the full benefits of our Himalayan salt in your lungs and on your skin. Great for all ages!

What are the benefits of Halo therapy?

Because the salt is ground to a microscopic consistency, it can penetrate the deepest recesses of your lungs and skin, which is where the real work takes place.Salt's antibacterial properties create an unwelcome environment for microbes within your body, all while avoiding irritation to the mucus membranes. It's a natural way to aid in the treatment of numerous illnesses, including, but not limited to: allergies, asthma, bronchitis, eczema, sinus infections and other chronic ear, nose, throat and respiratory conditions.
